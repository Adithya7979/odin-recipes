Pasta

Descripton

Making pasta can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar, spices and seasonings
  • Pasta noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the pasta.
  3. Make the sauce mixture.
  4. Mix the sauce and pasta according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Heat on simmer.
  6. Let the Pasta rest before serving.
